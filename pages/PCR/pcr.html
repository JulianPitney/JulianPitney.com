PCR Machine

Project Overview

Developed a point-of-care genetic screening device for an Ottawa biotech startup to detect genes linked to Alzheimer's risk. Led a team to prototype DNA purification and PCR amplification subsystems, with ongoing work on detection and integration.

My Contributions

  • Designed and implemented software and electronics:
    • Temperature sensing and PID control for thermal cycling.
    • Peltier heat pump and photodiode amplifier circuits.
    • Transport system for imaging and motorized valves for purification.
  • Collaborated with the chemistry team to meet hardware/software requirements.
